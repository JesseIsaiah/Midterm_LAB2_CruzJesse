Learning tech promises to make IT graduates more desirable to employers

MANILA – Analytics firm Stacktrek on Thursday said it built a learning tool to help enhance IT-based academic programs here in the Philippines. Information technology graduates in the country need better skills as 90 percent of them are "unhireable" based on a Stacktrek's study, CEO Bill Yuen told ANC’s The Briefing. "What we did is we created a technology, a learning management system that can help enhance the learning experience….It’s customized learning pathways to address weaknesses,"
